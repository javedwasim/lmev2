Questions often asked

Do I need a credit card to sign up for the free trial?

Yes, but only so that you can seamlessly begin using your account after your trial. You can cancel your account anytime during your trial or after you have started your subscription.

Can I cancel my account?

Yes, you can cancel your account at anytime with no hassle or cancel charges. If you wish to do so, please contact support@samcart.com.

Is there a refund policy?

Yes. After the trial period, you’re backed by our 30-day Double Double Guarantee. We’ll refund your money after the first 30 days if SamCart doesn’t double your sales.

Can I use different currencies?

Yes. We support the five major currencies including; USD, GBP, EUR, CAD, and AUD. Your customers will be charged in whichever currency you have your payment processor set to charge in.

Are there any fees?

Not at all. We don’t charge you or your customers any fees for anything. You are able to make any amount of sales for any amount of money without seeing any fees from SamCart.

Can I use coupons?

Yes, all of our checkout pages support coupons so you can boost your sales by giving your customers discounts.
